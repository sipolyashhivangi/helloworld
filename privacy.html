1. General

The privacy statement contained herein ("Privacy Statement") relates to the online information collection and use practices of FlexScore, Inc. in connection with www.FlexScore.com (the “Site”). FlexScore recognizes that many visitors and users of the Site are concerned about the information they provide to us and how we treat that information. This privacy statement has been developed to address those concerns.

2. Types Of Information Collected

When you visit or access the Site, you may provide us with the following types of information: (a) personally identifiable information, which is collected on an individual basis (such as during the registration process), (b) financial account information (such as information necessary for us to – per your request – pull your financial information for you to view on the Site), (c) information you otherwise elect to submit to the Site (such as when you post information for other Site users to see or engage in chat forums) and (d) general user information that does not contain personally identifiable information, which is collected on an aggregate basis. In some cases, if you choose not to provide us with requested information, you may not be able to access all of the Site Content or the Site's services.

2.1 Personally Identifiable Information. In order to register for the Site, and to fully enjoy the benefits of the FlexScore Service, you will be required to provide FlexScore with certain information, including your name, email address, date of birth, marital status, financial goals, assets and income, etc. ("Service Necessary Data"). If you elect not to provide certain types of Service Necessary Data – your use and enjoyment of the Site may be substantially limited.

2.2 General User Information. In addition to Service Necessary Data and User Submissions, we collect and record certain other data and information from and about you in connection with your use of the Site ("Usage Data"). For instance, similar to other commercial Web sites, the Site utilizes standard technology called "cookies" and Web server logs to collect information about how the Site is used.

2.3 Log Files - As is true of most web sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information may include Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and/or clickstream data.

Technologies such as: cookies, beacons, tags and scripts are used by FlexScore, Inc. and our partners Facebook and Twitter, affiliates, or analytics or service providers Google and MixPanel. These technologies are used in analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users’ movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We may receive reports based on the use of these technologies by these companies on an individual as well as aggregated basis.

We use cookies for analytics, site optimization, and tracking ad conversions. Users can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. If you reject cookies, you may still use our site, but your ability to use some features or areas of our site may be limited.

We partner with a third party to either display advertising on our Web site or to manage our advertising on other sites. Our third party partner may use technologies such as cookies to gather information about your activities on this site and other sites in order to provide you advertising based upon your browsing activities and interests.

(Google AdSense/Adwords) We use Google AdSense to publish ads on this site. When you view or click on an ad a cookie will be set to help better provide advertisements that may be of interest to you on this and other Web sites. You may opt-out of the use of this cookie by visiting Google’s Advertising and Privacy page: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

3. Disclosure/Use Of Information

3.1 Personally Identifiable Information. Service Necessary Data may be shared with (i) third parties who are contracted to perform services on behalf of FlexScore, such as those who assist FlexScore in bringing you the Site, (ii) third party financial consultants – per your request – so that they can provide you with financial services, and (iii) third party financial institutions as necessary for us to – per your request – pull your financial information for you to view/utilize on the Site. In addition, Service Necessary Data may be used by FlexScore internally: (i) in order to allow us to provide you with the products and services included within or accessible from the Site; and (ii) for our own marketing and/or research and development purposes. Except as otherwise noted in this Section 3.1, FlexScore will not rent or sell personally identifiable information we have collected from or about you without your permission.

3.2 User Submissions. User Submissions may be disclosed to the persons/groups elected by you through your selection of Site submission functionalities.

3.3 General User Information. We use Usage Data in the aggregate to better understand your use of the Site and to enhance your enjoyment and experience. For example, we may use such information to improve the design and content of the Site or to analyze the programs and services that we offer. We may share Usage Data with third parties to help improve our products and services or for other business purposes. Any Usage Data shared with any third parties will be disclosed as anonymized or aggregated data. From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically.

4. Other Disclosures

We will share your personal information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy policy. We may disclose your personally identifiable information as follows:

Service Providers and Agents: We employ other companies and people to perform tasks on our behalf. For example, parts of the Service may be hosted by a third party on our behalf or email service provider. Your information may be accessed by such providers/agents, in the event that we need to share your information with them to provide our services or customer support to you. Unless we tell you differently, FlexScore’s agents do not have any right to use information we share with them beyond what is necessary to assist us.

Business Transfers: In some cases, we may choose to buy or sell assets. In these types of transactions, customer information is typically one of the business assets that are transferred. Moreover, if FlexScore, or substantially all of its assets were acquired, or in the unlikely event that FlexScore goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, customer information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of FlexScore may continue to use your information as set forth in this policy. You will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our web site of any change in ownership or uses of your personally identifiable information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personally identifiable information.

Protection of FlexScore and Others: We may release your information when we believe in good faith that release is necessary to comply with the law, such as to comply with a subpoena; enforce or apply our conditions of use and other agreements; or protect the rights, property, or safety of FlexScore, our employees, our users, or others.

With Your Consent: Except as set forth above, you will be notified when your information may be shared with third parties, and will be able to prevent the sharing of this information.

5. Consent To Transfer

The Site is operated in the United States. If you are located outside of the United States, please be aware that any information you provide to us will be transferred to the United States. You consent to the transfer of your information and to the processing by of your information in the United States and in other countries and territories for the purposes set out in the Terms of Use in accordance with applicable law and regulations.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to maintain the security of the personally identifiable information that we collect, including limiting the number of people who have physical access to our database servers, as well as installing electronic security systems that guard against unauthorized access. When you enter sensitive information, such as a credit card number, we encrypt the transmission of that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL).

No data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure and we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information that you transmit to us, so you do so at your own risk. FlexScore has adequate procedures to safeguard records and information, consistent with Regulation S-P, and that information will not be disclosed to an unaffiliated third party for any reason, without your prior consent.

7. User Access and Choice

If your personally identifiable information changes, or if you no longer desire our service, you may correct, update, amend, delete/remove, or deactivate it by making the change on our member information page or by emailing our Customer Support at support@flexscore.com.

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

If you wish to subscribe to our newsletter(s), we will use your name and email address to send the newsletter to you. Out of respect for your privacy, you may choose to stop receiving our newsletter or marketing emails by following the unsubscribe instructions included in these emails, accessing the email preferences in your account settings page or you can contact us at support@flexscore.com.

For terminated users, user’s account data will be removed within 48 hours (except as otherwise required by law or regulation). If you decide to close your account(s) or become an inactive customer, our Privacy Policy will continue to apply to you.

Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.
